Humidity vs Latitude

Humidity does not seem strongly correlated with latitude. There does seem to be a small cluster of higher humidity between 0 Deg and -20 Deg. This is as likely to be the specific location of the measurements as anything directly related to latitude. There is also a cluster of 0 humidity data that seems unlikely, as it spans greater than 10 Degrees of latitude. This could be a desert, it would be necessary to determine the location of all measurements taken.
